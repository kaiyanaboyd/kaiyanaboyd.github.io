Resources

Here are a list of resources that can be used for help.

- Mindwise Innovations. A website where you can taking screenings.

- Teen Health & Wellness. A list of a hotlines for teenagers.

- N.A.M.I. A place for LGBTQI to recieve help.

- N.I.H. An informational website about women's mental health.

- A.D.A.A. A website that provides information on anxiety, depression, ocd, ptsd, & related disorders.

- Merick Manual. A website that shows different possible treatments of mental illnesses.

- Pysch Central. A website that provides a collection of mental health blogs.
